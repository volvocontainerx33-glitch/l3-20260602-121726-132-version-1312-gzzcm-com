His once-feared crime family has been dismantled, his territories seized by a coalition of Russian mobsters and a corrupt real estate mogul. Refusing a quiet retirement, Vincent assembles a small crew of loyal old-timers and disillusioned young outsiders. He doesn’t just want his empire back; he wants to reshape the rules of the game.
